Registrations Opens!

Hey everyone!

This is it! Registrations for the CSGames 2016 are now open!

Here are some important informations concerning the registrations :

  • 13/11/2015 : Registrations Open, one team per univeristy, others teams are on waiting list if you register more than one.
  • Until 15/12/2015 : EarlyBird price = 180$ per person
  • 15/12/2015 to 01/02/2016 : normal price = 200$ per person
  • 15/12/2015 : Registrations open for a second team
  • 01/02/2016 : Registrations close

Special Rule : Every team registered with at least 5 women out of 11 participants will benefit from the EarlyBird price, even after 15/12/2015.

Lore Guide

Rules of decent behaviour

  • Demonstrate civility, courtesy and respect for all individuals present (students, staff, sponsors, participants, organizers, volunteers, etc.)
  • Respect the equipment
  • Limit the space taken by your personal belongings to reasonable dimensions. (i.e. do not strew yourself)
  • It is strictly forbidden to bring or consume alcohol on campus at any time, including in opaque containers (insulated cup, coffee cup, etc.)
  • It is strictly forbidden to bring food or liquids in the Faculty’s classrooms
  • It is strictly forbidden to wander in the corridors and spaces not designated for the competition
